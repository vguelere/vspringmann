Olá, muito prazer!


Willian Murai, a.k.a Will Murai, was born in a small town in southeastern Brazil. The son of Japanese immigrants, has been a fan of anime, manga, and comic books since he was little kid. Inspired by the classic and contemporary artists in the industry, early on he knew that drawing is what he wanted to do for the rest of his life. While studying graphic design in college, he had the opportunity to work as a comic book colorist for American publishers, which earned him experience both in a professional and artistic aspect. After that, he decided to become an illustrator and, since then, he has been working in fields such as publishing, advertising, and internet, having recently dedicated special attention to game development and fantasy art. Some of his clients includes Wizards of the Coast, Crytek, Applibot, Ubisoft, Marvel Comics, Dinamite Entertainment, W/McCann, CUBOCC, LewLara TBWA, MRM Worldwide.
